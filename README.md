### 🛡️ About Me

I'm a Security Consultant and Penetration Tester with 2.5+ years of experience delivering **Web Application, API, Mobile, Network, and Active Directory** security assessments across enterprise and financial-sector environments. My work has covered everything from red team engagements against 1,000+ IP infrastructures to purple team exercises alongside SOC and Blue Team analysts.

I recently completed an **MSc in Cybersecurity (First Class Honours)** in Dublin, and I'm currently channeling that offensive-security background into building secure software — applying Secure SDLC, threat modelling, and Secure by Design principles as a developer, not just an attacker.

Right now I'm especially interested in the intersection of **AI and security** — how LLM-powered applications introduce new attack surfaces, and how to test and defend them.

- 🔭 Currently building **ForgeAI** — an open-source AI Security Learning Platform
- 🌱 Currently deepening my red team skills toward **CRTO (Certified Red Team Operator)**
- 🐛 Actively involved in bug bounty & responsible disclosure (Hall of Fame recognitions from Dell, Swiggy, BigBasket, and others)
- 💬 Ask me about penetration testing, Active Directory attacks, threat modelling, or secure SDLC
- 📍 Based in Dublin, Ireland
